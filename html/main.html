About me...

A software engineering and management student, with a drive to always learn something new, expand my knowledge on what I know, and make connections with great people along the way.

My background

My journey as a software engineer began in my first year in high school. I grew up in the Florida Keys where, at least in 2011, programming was not a common subject to hear or even learn about. One of my elective courses was computer science, which at the time all I knew of was 1's and 0's, but I soon realized it was much more than that. After that year I had the basic understanding of how computers worked and had scraped the surface of how to start coding. The following year I signed up for a web design course where I was introduced to HTML/CSS and JavaScript. This is also the year where I discovered about different ways to teach myself how to program, beginning with CodeAcademy. From there I was introduced to different languages such as Python, Ruby, PHP and Java. After finding this new niche I kept trying to teach myself as much as I could, but eventually I was unable to go further. I took the remainder of my high school career to focus on maintaining my GPA, helping run school clubs, as well as completing my Eagle Scout project.
